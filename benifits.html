Benefits of Solar Cooking

Zero Operating Cost

A solar oven uses sunlight instead — the sun doesn't send a bill! Once you have the oven, every meal cooked with it has zero fuel cost. Sources like Solar Cookers International, Wikipedia, and various eco/expert sites emphasize this: "Solar cooking has zero cost for fuel" and "cost nothing to operate." No electricity or gas consumption — No plugging in, no gas connection, no meter running. This makes it truly free to run during sunny hours, ideal in sunny Kathmandu (plenty of clear days year-round, especially Oct–May).

Reduces deforestation

They cook using free solar energy—no wood, charcoal, or other biomass needed. When a household uses a solar oven for one or more meals (especially lunch on sunny days), they burn less or no firewood for those meals.

No external fuel dependency

In places with unreliable LPG/electricity or high fuel costs, sunlight is truly "free forever" after the initial setup.
